The First Popular Mac

Luke Dormehl. "Today in Apple history: The final Apple II model arrives" Cult of Mac, Sept 15, 2018, Cult of Mac, Apple IIc. Accessed 22 Sept. 2023

This new version of the Mac had a very controversial change going from a 5.25in floppy drive to a 3.5in. This computer sold an average of 100,000 units over a 4-year period.
